VILLA BLEU

Description

In Sidi Bou Said, this blue and white village, you will discover while strolling this beautiful large traditional and residential house built in 1991. Thanks to the famous architect Tarek Ben Miled, it combines Arab and Andalusian style. Designer Edoardo Palermo has created a unique decoration in a fresh contemporary style, chosen to get along with old ceramics and authentic materials. Each of the 13 rooms and suites enjoys a magnificent sea view and offers you a perfect balance between tradition and modernity. You will also appreciate the romantic lounge facing the sea and the gourmet restaurant renowned for its fine and creative cuisine with international dishes.
